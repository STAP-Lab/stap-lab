Focus areas

Multi-Modal Generative AI, Security & Privacy, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Internet of Things

Main research topics

Secure and Trustworthy AI for Autonomous Systems

Security: Protecting AI systems and data from adversarial attacks.

Interpretability: Making AI decisions transparent and understandable to ensure trust and responsible deployment.

Robustness: Ensuring models perform reliably under diverse data distribution or unexpected environmental conditions.

Generative AI-powered CPS/IoT Applications

Developing responsible, adaptive, efficient and deployable generative AI agents in CPS/IoT applications such as mobility, manufacturing and smart home.

Multi-Modal and Collaborative AI Systems

Developing AI systems that integrate diverse modalities. Enabling coordination, communication, and collaborative decision-making among networked AI systems.
