PhD Student & Research Intern

We are looking for highly motivated PhD students and research interns to join our lab working on cutting-edge research in Physical AI, Generative AI Agents, AI Security, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Internet of Things. Students will conduct original research, publish in top-tier venues, and collaborate on interdisciplinary projects with both academic and industry impact. We seek students with strong backgrounds in machine learning, robotics, computer vision, cybersecurity, or related areas. Please contact yzhu39@wayne.edu and email your CV.
